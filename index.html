About

California High School Fightin' Grizzlies Robotics competes in the VEX Robotics universe, but that's not where the story began. In 2005, a group of students led the robotics club in the FIRST competition, and eventually won regionals in 2007, but competing in FIRST was ended in 2012 for issues of cost. However, today Cal High competes under the VEX name, where we have made a name for ourselves with outstanding designs. Right now, the club is led by Nicholas Swan, and the advisor is Mr. Reed. Mr. Reed can regale you with tales of the old league from when Cal competed in the FIRST world. See more about the competition teams on their specific pages.
