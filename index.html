about us

California High School Fightin' Grizzlies robotics competes in the VEX universe. Initially started by a group of high schoolers in 2005, Cal High competed in FIRST robotics, where we won regionals in 2007, but later switched into VEX in 2012. Now, we have made a name for ourselves with outstanding designs.
