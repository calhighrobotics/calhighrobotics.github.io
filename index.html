about us

california high school fightin' grizzlies robotics competes in the vex universe. Initially started by a group of highschoolers in 2005, cal high competed in first robotics, where we won regionals in 2007, but later switched into vex in 2012. now, we have made a name for ourselves with outstanding designs.
