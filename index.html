ABOUT US

We're a robotics team located in the East Bay Area, California.

California High School Fightin' Grizzlies Robotics competes in the VEX universe. Initially started by a group of highschoolers in 2005, Cal High competed in FIRST robotics. We won regionals in 2007, but later switched into VEX in 2012. Now, we have made a name for ourselves with outstanding designs.
